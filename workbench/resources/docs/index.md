# Acme Ledger

Welcome to **Acme Ledger** — double-entry accounting that your engineers will
actually enjoy integrating with. This documentation is powered by
[Docent](https://github.com/stechstudio/laravel-docent), so it adapts to who you
are and what your account can do.

:::tip title="New here?"
Start with the [installation guide](getting-started/installation), then run
through the [quickstart](getting-started/quickstart) to record your first
transaction in about five minutes.
:::

## What you can do

- Record income and expenses with a clean, typed API.
- Reconcile against connected bank feeds automatically.
- Generate investor-ready reports on demand.
- Manage billing, plans, and seats without leaving your app.

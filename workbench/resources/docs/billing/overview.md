# Billing Overview

Billing on Acme Ledger is usage-based. You are charged for the transactions you
post and the seats you use.

You are currently on the **{{ value:account.plan }}**.

## What counts as usage

- Posted transactions (reconciliations are free).
- Active seats during the billing period.
- Report exports beyond the monthly allowance.

:::info title="Fair-use pooling"
Unused transaction allowance rolls over for one billing period, so a quiet month
cushions a busy one.
:::

Manage your subscription any time from your
[billing settings]({{ link:billing.settings }}).

Continue to [payment methods](payment-methods) to add or update a card.
